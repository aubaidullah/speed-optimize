FAQ
What is the best time to visit Tamil Nadu for a family vacation?
Tamil Nadu can be visited round the year but avoid coastal area during summers.
Which are the major town in Tamil Nadu ?
Pondicheery, Chennai are the metro town with international connectivity by regular flights . Tirupati, Mahabalipuram, Kanchipuram are other major town which are well connected to the rest of India by train and flights
What are the must visit tourist destinations in Tamil Nadu ?
Tirupati, Mahabalipuram, Madurai, Pondicheery, Chennai, Kanchipuram, Rameshwaram .
Which are the famous temples in Tamil Nadu ?
Sri Ekambarnathar Temple, Meenakshi Temple,Arunachaleeswarar temple,Brahadhiswara Temple,Rameshwaram Jyotirlinga Temple,Sarangapani Temple.
What are the languages spoken in Tamil Nadu?
Main language are Tamil but most of the local people understand English and Hindi also.

About Southern Temples Tour
Tamil Nadu is bordered by the Bay of Bengal, Indian Ocean, the Gulf of Mannar, the Eastern Ghats, the Nilgiri Mountains and the Meghmalai Hills. Such a strategically located place can only have the best of natural views and scenic beauty! Plan the best tour in Tamil Nadu to include all the coastal areas, the hill stations and the places of religious significance. The perfect start to the journey is the capital city of Chennai. Along with awesome beaches, Marina Beach especially and Tamil architecture, it is a shopping destination famous for the South Indian silk saris. The other best places to visit in your itenary should include Coimbatore, Tiruchirappalli, Salem, Vellore, Thoothukudi and Tirunelveli. Some of the best sites in the state are the World Heritage Sites of Brihadisvara Temple at Thanjavur, the Airavatesvara Temple at Darasuram, the Shore Temple on the Bay of Bengal, the Seven Pagodas at Mamallapuram, Pancha Rathas at the Coromandel Coast and the 11 Mandapas. Another engineering wonder and structural beauty stamped by UNESCO as a World Heritage Site is the Nilgiri Mountain Railways that runs across the mountainous terrain and through thick forests.
Tourists who are travelling for religious and spiritual discoveries must come to Tamil Nadu that has more than 34000 Hindu temples. From rock relief images and sculptures to the Monolith cave temple built by the Pandyas in Kalugumalai to the Kalugasalamoorthy temple dedicated to Murugan all are architectural wonders. The rock-cut caves at Sittanavasal, the Tamil-Brahmi inscriptions on the Samanar Malai, the Kailashnathar Temple at Kanchipuram are all worth a visit. The city of Kumbakonam with 188 temples and the pilgrimage towns of Palani, Nagore, Rameshwaram, Madurai, Srirangam and Tiruchendur are must-visit places. One of the best travel experiences is the visit to the tip of mainland of India, Kanyakumari. The Vivekananda Rock Memorial is a unique experience. Some of the best activities for the daring and bravehearts include treks and camps across the Nilgiri Mountains, Pear orchards and beaches of Tamil Nadu. The popular hill stations of Ooty, Kodaikanal and Coonor offer mesmerizing views, the perfect appetizer to the soul. more... less..
