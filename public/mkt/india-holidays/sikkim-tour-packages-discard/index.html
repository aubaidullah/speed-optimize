About Sikkim
Gangtok, one of the famous tourist destinations is the capital of the Himalayan Sikkim state. Buddhist monasteries, beautiful atmosphere sceneries, and a great cuisine are some of the key aspects for which Gangtok is well-known. The panoramic view of snow-clad Mt. Kanchenjunga adds beauty to the hill station of Gangtok. Take a dip in the breathtaking Tsomgo Lake, nestled near Gangtok on the Gangtok-Nathu La highway. The lake is surrounded by the rich green landscapes. Another major attraction is Baba Mandir, located near to Tsomgo Lake. The temple of Baba Harbhajan Singh has a great holy importance and that is why visited by hundreds of people daily. Take a pit stop here and listen the tales associated with this place
 The hill town of Darjeeling never fails to amaze tourists with its beautiful calm monasteries, rich green hills, tea estates, and the wonderful toy train. Darjeeling is known in the world for tea. Your trip to Darjeeling is incomplete if you don?t sip the hot cuppa tea. Leave early morning for Tiger Hill to watch the splendid sunrise view and have a panoramic view of Mt. Kanchenjunga and Mount Everest together. People visit here in a large number daily especially for the sunrise view. Ghoom Monastery, Batasia Loop, Padwaja Naidu Himalayan Zoological Park, Himalayan Mountaineering Institute, and Tenzing & Gumbo Rock are some of the other major must visit attractions. Enjoy the mystical game of the mist and fog all the time when you are in Darjeeling. more... less..
