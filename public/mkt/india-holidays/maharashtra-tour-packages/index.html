Which are the major town in Maharshtra ?
Mumbai, Nashik, Aurangabad, Shirdi are the metro town with international connectivity by regular flights. Nagpur, Mahabaleshwar, Sagareshwar are other major town which are well connected to the rest of India by train.

About Maharashtra
Maharashtra, located on the western part of the Deccan plateau is one of the richest states of India. From the tourism point, there are ample places here that offer unforgettable travel experiences to travelers. The state boasts of not only the capital city Mumbai that's considered the financial and entertainment capital of India and has some awesome beaches and nightlife; Maharashtra has the world heritage sites of Ajanta and Ellora caves, the fort of Devgiri and the Bibi Ka Maqbara at Aurangabad known for its resemblance to the Taj Mahal of Agra. Some of the best places to visit here include the British built hill-stations of Mahabaleshwar and Matheran. Shirdi is frequented by the followers of Sai baba while the Gurudwara of Hazur Sahib at Nanded is a pious place for the Sikh community. The nature reserves of Melghat and Tadoba-Andhari are under the Tiger Reserve project of the country. The Navegaon National Park at Gondia is a well-known bird sanctuary.
Adventure and nature lovers and seekers can indulge in best activities at Amba Ghat jungle safari; overnight camping at Lonavala; trekking of Malhargad, Jejuri and Purandar and catch sight of the Sonori fort and ancient temples. Indulge yourself in scuba diving at the beaches of the Konkan area and visit age-old palaces and forts. Catch the beauty of the underwater-sea life while parasailing and scuba diving at the Malvan beach in Tarkali. Some of the other notable escapades include visit to Alibaug; the pristine Igatpuri where adventure activities like rappelling, rock climbing, and trekking can also be done by tourists; the Umbrella Falls at Bhandardara; the blue water and golden sand of Kashid; the scenically beautiful Khandala; Panchgani in Satara district; the picturesque town of Ratnagiri surrounded by the Arabian Sea on one side and Sahyadri range on the other; the Lakeside Promenade at Lavasa; Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaja's Raigad Fort; the religious city of Nashik; the Dabdaba Falls at Jawhar in the Thane district; the serene and not-so-known site of Malshej Ghat; the flora and fauna of Toranmal; the Pawna Lake and Sinhagad Fort at Pune the list is unending for interested tourists. more... less..
